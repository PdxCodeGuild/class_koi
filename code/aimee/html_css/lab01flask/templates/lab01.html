Y'shtola Rhul

Archon, sorceress, Scion

"I will join the fray when I've finished my tea."

About

Y'shtola Rhul is a Miqo'te affiliated with the Seeker of the Sun Tribe. She hails from the warm lands of La Noscea, and is a preeminent member of the Scions of the Seventh Dawn. She is a master of the arcane arts, having mastery in both conjuring as well as sorcery. At age seven, she was sent to study under the tutelage of the famous Archon Matoya, and after ten years, joined the Archon arcane experts herself.

She is serious and scholarly, though she is steadfast and dedicated to her friends. Y'shtola claims to be 23, thought to perhaps be the age at when she stopped growing older. Aside from scholarly pursuits, her biggest interest is collecting shoes. She has a great dislike for aureliae.
